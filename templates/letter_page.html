Your exciting journey starts here!

If you are wondering how to start writing your letter. Here are some ideas for you:

  1. Remind Yourself That Little Things Matter
    We are always worried about the big events in your lives, but what about a cup of coffee in the mornings, the smell of a new book, or a small talk with your neighbor? These things will be nice to remember when you receive the letter in the future.
  2. Next Birthday
    Birthdays are so special. They remind us that we are growing older, although they symbolize how far we have come. They give everyone a day to feel special. and putting your wishes within a letter it is a nice to way to gift someone or yourself.
  3. To Your Future Grandchild
    Tell them how the world looks like now and share your thoughts and advice about climate change in our lifetimes.
  4. Talking About Who You Are Know
    You can choose an age that puts you in a different circunstances than you are currently in. When you read the letter you will be able to see how much your life has changed and whether or not your goals have been met.
  5. To The Year 2040
    Maybe the air you breathe will be cleaner and the Nature will be recovering. Saving the climate involves huge change and you can start these changes by writing down your thoughts and wishes!
